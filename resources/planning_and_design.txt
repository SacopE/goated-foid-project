=========================
APP PLANNING & DESIGN
=========================

1. PROJECT IDEA
-------------------------
- What is your app called? Awesome Workout App
- What type of app is it? (journal, listings, notes, etc.) It’s a fitness tracking app, so more about listing and journalling
- What will users use your app for? To track weight training progress.

2. DATA MODEL (WHAT ARE YOU STORING?)
-------------------------
- What is a single “entry” in your app? Bench PR
- What fields will each entry have? (title, content, date, etc.) Weight and PR type
- How will entries be connected to a user? They are personalized to the user’s input

3. CORE FEATURES (CRUD)
-------------------------
- What can users CREATE? A new Entry
- What will they see on the main page (READ)? A list of their PRs
- What can users EDIT (UPDATE)? They can edit their PRs
- What can users DELETE? They can delete their PRs

4. USER FLOW
-------------------------
- What happens after a user logs in? They are greeted by a page that says welcome and add exercise
- What pages/routes will your app need? (dashboard, create, edit, etc.) They will need a create, edit, and delete route
- Describe a typical user experience from login → using your app Upon logging in the user just creates a new exercise and after that they can edit, create, or delete an exercise

5. USER EXPERIENCE (UX)
-------------------------
- How should your app look and feel? It looks simplistic and sleek without anything distracting the user.
- How will users navigate between pages? There are text links that lead to the different pages.
- How will entries be displayed (list, cards, etc.)? They are displayed in cards on a dashboard once the user logs in.

6. OPTIONAL FEATURES
-------------------------
- What extra features might you add? (search, tags, sorting, etc.) Images describing the exercises or videos attached to the boxes so that there’s proof of the PR.
